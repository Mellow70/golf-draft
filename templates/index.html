How to Play

  • Join the draft starting at 8 PM EDT on June 12, 2025!
  • Log in with your assigned username and pick only when it’s your turn.
  • Each pick has a 3-minute timer—miss it, and an auto-pick grabs the top-ranked golfer.
  • Choose 3 golfers total, tracked in the Players and Picks table.
  • The winner takes all; if no golfer wins, the highest rank decides.
  • Enjoy the competition!
